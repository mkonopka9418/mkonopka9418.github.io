Personal Projects

I have been interested in programming since I was 12 years old, so I have created a number of personal projects over the years. Some were for school, some were not, but all of them were fun to work on! See some of them highlighted below.

Ball and Chain

A simple game where you control a flying ship equipped with a ball and chain and try to protect your castle from all the incoming enemies!

Mines Sweeper

A clone of the classic Windows Minesweeper game. I made this for a school project just for fun. Differently than the original, in this version you can make completely customize the grid size and the number of mines that appear.

Solar System Simulator

A 3D simulation of the planets in our solar system. You can see the earth on the blue ring, and if you look closely, you'll see the moon orbiting it as well. Use the controls to explore the entire solar system at your leisure!
